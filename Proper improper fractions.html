Fraction operations

Proper fractions

When the numerator(top part) of a fraction is lesser than the denominator(lower part), then the fraction is proper. For example, 5/6, 7/8, 3/6, etc.

Improper fractions

When the numerator(top part) of a fraction is greater than or equal to the denominator(lower part), then the fraction is improper. For example, 6/5, 8/7, 3/3, etc.
