Reciprocals

The reciprocal of a fraction is also known as the multiplicative inverse of a fraction. It is basically the numerator and denominator exchanged. For example:-
Reciprocal of 3/5 is 5/3.
Reciprocal of 6/1 is 1/6.
Reciprocal of 35/10 is 10/35.
0 does not have a reciprocal.
