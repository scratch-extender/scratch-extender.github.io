Equivalent fractions

If 2 fractions are equal, they are equivalent. So if you take a fraction and multiply or divide its numerator and denominator with the same number, they are equal.
3/5 is equivalent to 6/10, 9/15, 12/20, etc.
4/2 is equivalent to 2/1, 8/4, etc.
1/1 is equivalent to 2/2, 100/100, 5/5, etc. Here's a video to better understand equivalent fractions.
