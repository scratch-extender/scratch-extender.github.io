Like and unlike fractions

Like fractions

When the denominators (the bottom numbers) of two fractions are the same, they are called like fractions. For example, 5/6 and 3/6, 7/8 and 9/8, 4/9 and 9/9, etc.

Unlike fractions

Unlike fractions are fractions where the denominators are not the same. For example, 6/5 and 8/7, 1/2 and 3/4, etc.
